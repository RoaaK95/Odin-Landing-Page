Volunteer with Penguins

Penguins are sadly one of the most threatened groups of seabirds, with half of the 18 species listed by Birdlife as either Vulnerable or Endangered. Volunteer with penguins and take your chance to learn more about these cute little birds.

How Are Penguins Endangered?

10 of the world's 18 penguin species are endangered

In 2022, thousands of young penguins have been killed by ice breakup

Emperor penguins may be extinct by 2100 due to Climate Change

African penguins are on track for extinction by 2035
